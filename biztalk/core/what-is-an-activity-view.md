## <a name="activity-view-elements"></a><span data-ttu-id="38698-109">Elementos de la vista de actividad</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38698-109">Activity view elements</span></span>

-   <span data-ttu-id="38698-113">Orígenes de eventos</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38698-113">Event sources</span></span>  
  
-   <span data-ttu-id="38698-114">Orígenes de datos</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38698-114">Data sources</span></span>  
  
 <span data-ttu-id="38698-115">**Hitos**: los hitos son objetos que definen un punto en un proceso determinado.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38698-115">**Milestones**: Milestones are objects which define a point in a given process.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="38698-116">Existen tres formas de obtener acceso a los hitos:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="38698-116">Milestones are accessed in one of three ways:</span></span>

> [!NOTE]
